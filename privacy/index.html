Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 April 2026

ProxmoxVue is a client app. It has no backend. It does not collect, store, or transmit your data to us or to any third party.

Short version: We never see anything about you or your Proxmox server. The app runs entirely on your iPad. There are no accounts, no analytics, no cloud relay.

What data the app handles

When you add a Proxmox host, the following is stored on your device:

  • The host's display name, URL, and TLS preference (SwiftData — iOS on-device storage).
  • Your authentication credentials — API token, or username + password, depending on which method you chose. Stored in the iOS Keychain, accessible only to this app and only while the device is unlocked.
  • For OIDC logins (e.g. Authentik): the session ticket and CSRF token are held in memory only — never persisted. A new browser-login is required if the session expires.

What leaves your device

ProxmoxVue communicates directly and exclusively with the Proxmox server(s) you add. Specifically:

  • Standard Proxmox VE REST API calls (/api2/json/...) using the credentials you configured.
  • For OIDC: one browser session with your identity provider (e.g. Authentik, Keycloak) to obtain an authorization code, which is exchanged with your Proxmox server.

Nothing is sent to any other party.

What we collect

Nothing. We run no servers for ProxmoxVue. We have no analytics, no crash-reporting SDK, no advertising identifier. The App Store provides aggregate download statistics to us, but those contain no personal data about you.

Third-party services

The app makes no calls to any third-party service on our behalf. If you choose an OIDC realm, the browser login you trigger will contact your configured identity provider — that contract is between you and them, not us.

Your Proxmox server

Your Proxmox server logs its own API calls. Review its logging and retention policies directly with the administrator (which may be you).

Children

ProxmoxVue is not directed at children under 13. It's a system administration tool.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, the app update notes will mention it and this page will be updated with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact
