Support

Last updated: 19 April 2026

Have a question, a bug, or a feature request? Mail us.

Email
support@proxmoxvue.app

Before you write

Check the FAQ first — it covers authentication, self-signed TLS, versions, and roadmap questions.

Reporting a bug

Include as much of the following as you can:

  • What you did — the steps that produced the issue.
  • What you expected — and what actually happened.
  • App version + build — bottom of Settings → About.
  • iPadOS version — Settings → General → About on your iPad.
  • Proxmox VE version — e.g. 8.2.7.
  • Auth method — API token, username + password, or OIDC (with provider name).
  • Screenshots or screen-recordings if the bug is visual.

Feature requests

We read them all. Tell us what you're trying to do and why — the use case matters more than the exact implementation.

Security reports

If you think you've found a security issue, please mail security@proxmoxvue.app rather than posting publicly. We'll acknowledge within a few business days.
